lir - "lehr- und lernsystem information retrieval"

lir is an experimental retrieval environment to teach/learn information retrieval concepts. it's designed to be highly configurable and parametrisable.

lir.pl is the retrieval front-end:
> lir.pl

lir_index.pl is the web-frontend for creating indexes for lir.pl
> lir_index.pl (restricted access)
